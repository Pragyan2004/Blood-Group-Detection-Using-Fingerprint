About This Project

Fingerprint Blood Group Prediction is an AI-based system that determines blood groups from fingerprint images.

How It Works

  • Users upload a fingerprint image.
  • The image is preprocessed and analyzed using deep learning.
  • A trained AI model predicts the blood group.

Why Use This System?

Fast & Accurate

Uses advanced deep learning models for quick and reliable predictions.

Non-Invasive

Determines blood group without requiring blood samples.

Easy to Use

Simply upload an image, and the system does the rest.
